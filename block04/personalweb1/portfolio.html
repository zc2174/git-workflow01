Here are my projects!

Project 1

RecipeRevolution is a culinary app born from a passion for global cuisine. Merging AI and user-generated content, it suggests unique recipe variations based on ingredients at hand. The AI analyzes user preferences, dietary restrictions, and pantry items to offer personalized meal ideas. Beyond mere recipes, it tells the cultural story behind dishes, bringing a world of flavors to the user's kitchen. Its collaborative platform encourages users to tweak and share their adaptations, fostering a community of global food enthusiasts. RecipeRevolution isn't just about cooking—it's a journey of taste and culture.

Project 2

EcoThreads is a sustainable fashion initiative, challenging fast fashion's wastefulness. Harnessing the power of upcycling, this project transforms discarded garments into chic, wearable art. By collaborating with local artists, we breathe new life into old clothing, creating unique pieces that tell a story. The accompanying digital platform features DIY tutorials, enabling users to embark on their own upcycling adventures. It also hosts a marketplace for artists to showcase and sell their creations. EcoThreads is more than just fashion; it's a movement towards conscious consumption, proving that style doesn't have to come at the planet's expense.

Project 3

MindMingle is a virtual reality (VR) meditation space, designed to enhance relaxation and mindfulness. Venturing beyond traditional meditation apps, this platform leverages VR technology to transport users to customizable, serene environments – from tranquil beaches to serene mountaintops. Accompanied by ambient soundscapes and guided sessions, users can immerse themselves in a multi-sensory meditation experience. The platform also integrates biofeedback sensors, adjusting visual and auditory elements based on the user's heart rate and breathing, ensuring a tailored relaxation journey. MindMingle is not just an escape from reality, but a deeper dive into self-awareness and inner peace.
